## Responsibility

The Dashboard component represents the main landing page of the application, designed as an interactive space-themed galaxy.
- It displays the five 5th-grade California Common Core math topics as distinct "planets".
- It handles rendering topic states based on whether they are `locked`, `unlocked`, or `mastered` (with a trophy icon).
- It lists stats such as current star count, streak, and success rates for each specific planet, allowing users to select an unlocked topic to begin learning and practicing.
