## Responsibility

The Visual Aids component renders dynamic SVG/HTML interactive visual aids matching the active math problem to help students visualize concepts:
- **Coordinate Grid:** An interactive Cartesian coordinate system showing axes, labels, grid lines, and interactive point plotting for geometry standards (`5.G.1`).
- **Decimal Blocks:** Grid-based visualization showing 10x10 grids for representing decimals (`5.NBT.7`).
- **Pie Charts:** SVG circle charts split into fractional segments representing fractions (`5.NF.1`).
- **Base-10 Blocks:** Visual representation of units, rods, flats, and cubes for place value and powers of 10 (`5.NBT.1`).
